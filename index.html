Our Story

More than a meal — it's an experience

Bella Cucina was born from a simple idea: bring the warmth of an Italian grandmother's kitchen to our city. Chef Marco Rossi spent 12 years cooking across Florence, Naples, and Rome before opening our doors in 2009.

Every dish is made from scratch using imported DOP ingredients, fresh local produce, and recipes passed down for generations.
